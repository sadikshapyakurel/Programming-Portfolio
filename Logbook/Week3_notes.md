## Summary of Week 3 Lecture

This week focused on **Control Statements in Python**, which are used to **control program flow** by making decisions and repeating tasks.We learned about **Boolean expressions** that evaluate to **True or False** and are used in decision making. The lecture covered **conditional statements** such as **`if`**, **`elif`**, and **`else`**.We also studied **logical operators** (**`and`**, **`or`**, **`not`**) and **membership operators** (**`in`**, **`not in`**) to create complex conditions.Finally, we practiced **loops** using **`while`** and **`for`**, including loop control statements like **`break`**, **`continue`**, and **nested control statements**.
